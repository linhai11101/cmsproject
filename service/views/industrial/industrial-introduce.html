What is the introduction of industrial cluster certification?

"Global Procurement Base" certification,developed and dominated by Mr-world.com for industrial clusters which have reached considerable capacity scale and quality standards in production, trade and other aspects,has been audited and commissioned by the global authority of the certification body Switzerland SGS company and awarded an international certification authority."Global procurement base" certification content

1. Product quality system: a comprehensive assessment of enterprise quality control capabilities, research and development capabilities, cost management and after- sales service capabilities.

2. Enterprise comprehensive ability: including human resources, production capacity,export capacity, foreign trade capabilities, social responsibility.

3. Operational capacity: a comprehensive assessment of the actual operation of the enterprise indicators, such as the average delivery, product qualification rate, customer complaints rate, the proportion of big buyers and so on.

4. Qualification inspection: including: business license, tax registration certificate, the organization code inspection.

5. Certificate inspection: including product certification certificate inspection, system certification inspection.
